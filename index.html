Swarm Survivors - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Swarm Survivors does not collect, store, or sell any personal user data on its own servers.

Google Play Games Services

The game uses Google Play Games Services for features such as leaderboards and authentication. Google may process basic information such as:

  • Game profile ID
  • Leaderboard scores
  • Basic gameplay activity required for ranking

This data is managed by Google and not stored by the developer.

In-App Purchases

The game may include optional in-app purchases (e.g. coins). These are processed securely by Google Play Billing.

Data Deletion

Although Swarm Survivors does not store user data directly, users can request deletion of their Google Play Games data via the official Google account settings or Play Games profile.
